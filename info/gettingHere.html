Getting to the venue

Due to a strike, Metro services will be suspended from 6.30am to 10am on the 9th and 11th of September. Please check alternative modes below.

The DTN Annual Forum 2025 will be hosted at Institute of Geography and Spatial Planning (IGOT). It is located in the University of Lisbon campus in heart of Lisbon city, accessible by several transport modes.

This page specifies the transport interfaces that are closest to the DTN venue. For more details on Lisbon transportation system network, apps and prices, refer to Getting around.

By bike

IGOT is accessible through the city cycle lane infrastructure.

There is a station from the public bike system right outside the venue. Its number is 461 - Rua Professor Teresa Ambrósio. Find the stations map at GIRA website.

You can also park an e-scooter or any other shared mobility vehicle right outside. They are allowed inside the campus.

By train/metro

IGOT is just a 15 minutes walk from Entrecampos interface. There you can catch an urban train to almost anywhere in Lisbon metropolitan area.

This interface also connects to metro. There you can catch the yellow line and easily connect to the others (2 stations away from green and red lines, 4 from the blue line).

Regarding metro, the stippness of the terrain in this area makes Entrecampos a better option to leave the venue. To get there, Cidade Universitária metro station is a better option.

By bus

Urban buses accept contactless card payment :)

IGOT is served by several lines of the urban bus operator, Carris, connecting to almost anywhere in the city: 701, 731, 732, 735, 738, 754, 755, 764 and 768.

There is also a suburban bus that serves it, 3702 from operator Carris Metropolitana, connecting to Almada, in the south bank of Lisbon metro area.
